About Me

I'm Michael — known as "Meadxr" in the esports world. Austin-raised, now based in Denton, TX, with 10 years of competitive FPS experience across Overwatch, VALORANT, CS2, and Deadlock. That background gave me a ground-level understanding of what it actually takes to improve, which is what eventually led me into coaching.

My Story

How I got here

Growing up in Austin, I found my way into competitive gaming early — spending years climbing the ranks in Overwatch before branching out into VALORANT, CS2, and Deadlock. That competitive mindset is what eventually shaped everything else I do.

Since 2023 I've channeled that experience into coaching through EPA.gg, working with collegiate programs and individual players. On the creative side, I'm self-taught across the Adobe suite — Premiere Pro, Photoshop, and After Effects — editing for creators and managing social media for esports organizations.

I'm finishing a BBA in Marketing at the University of North Texas, and I'm building toward a career that connects all of it — the competitive knowledge, the creative work, and the marketing background.
