About

A bit about me

I'm Michael — a designer, content creator, and esports coach based in Denton, TX, with over 5 years of competitive experience across Overwatch, VALORANT, and Deadlock. That background reaching the highest ranks in multiple FPS titles gave me a ground-level understanding of what it actually takes to improve.

Since 2023 I've channeled that into coaching through EPA.gg, working with collegiate programs and individual players. On the creative side, I'm self-taught across the Adobe suite — Premiere Pro, Photoshop, and After Effects — editing for creators and managing social media for esports organizations.

I'm finishing a BBA in Marketing at the University of North Texas, and I'm building toward a career that connects all of it — the competitive knowledge, the creative work, and the marketing background.
