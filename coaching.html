Where I Coach

Esports Performance Academy

EPA is a professional coaching and player development platform founded in 2019 by Andrew Cooley — a former pro-level competitor in Counter-Strike and World of Warcraft, and a League of Legends pro team coach. Seeing a gap in how players were developed both in-game and beyond it, Andrew built EPA to set a higher standard.

EPA connects experienced coaches with players and collegiate esports programs, providing structure, accountability, and a development framework that goes beyond just gameplay. Think of it like a professional coaching agency for esports — similar in concept to platforms like Metafy, but with a deeper focus on long-term player growth.

I work under EPA as a coach, serving clients and collegiate programs they bring on. My sessions are booked and managed through their platform, giving players a professional experience from start to finish.

The Process

How a session works

Every player is different, but the structure stays consistent — review, refine, repeat.

01

Film Review

We start by reviewing your VODs together — breaking down decision-making, positioning, and mechanics to identify exactly what's holding you back.

02

Live Session

1-on-1 coaching during live play. Real-time feedback while the moment is fresh, so the lesson sticks instead of getting lost after the fact.

03

Growth Plan

Clear takeaways and focus areas to carry into your next games — so improvement compounds session over session.
